The Cairo TV Tower ( Arabic The price is right Burj al-Qāhira ( Egyptian-Arabic: Burg el-Qāhira ) is a 187-meter-high television tower of solid construction , whose design is reminiscent of lotus flowers . The Cairo TV Tower was completed in 1961 by 500 workers on the Nile island of Gezira and has an observation deck open to the public, a café and a revolving restaurant on three levels . The diameter of the tower shaft is 14 meters. The special feature of the Cairo TV Tower is that it is made of pure concrete without a steel skeleton, making it the tallest building made of pure concrete.

An Egyptian sphinx ( plural : sphinxes or sphinxes ) is a statue of a lion , usually with a human head. Ram , falcon and sparrowhawk heads are also common. The most famous is the Great Sphinx of Giza . Other ancient peoples depicted similar hybrid creatures , including the Phoenicians , Hittites , Assyrians and especially the Greeks .
